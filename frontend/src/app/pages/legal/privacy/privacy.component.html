2. Legal Basis for Data Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds under GDPR Article 6:

  • Contract Performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations for your accommodation booking
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): Compliance with German Federal Registration Act (BMG § 29) and tax laws
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Fraud prevention, security, and service improvement
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Marketing communications (where applicable and with your explicit consent)

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data during the check-in and booking process:

3.1 Guest Check-In Data

  • First name and last name
  • Email address (verified via 6-digit code)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Government-issued identification document (passport or national ID card) - uploaded image

3.2 Booking Information

  • Reservation code
  • Check-in and check-out dates

3.3 Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Access times and dates

4. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

4.1 Service Provision and Contract Fulfillment

  • Processing and managing your accommodation bookings
  • Facilitating online check-in and identity verification
  • Generating access codes and QR codes for property access
  • Communicating with you about your reservation
  • Processing payments and issuing invoices

4.2 Legal Compliance

  • Guest registration and identity verification as required by German Federal Registration Act (BMG § 29)
  • Tax reporting and tourism tax collection (where applicable)
  • Responding to legal requests from authorities when required by law

4.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Verifying guest identity through email verification and ID document upload
  • Protecting our platform and users from security threats
  • Preventing unauthorized access to properties

All third-party processors are contractually bound to GDPR compliance and process data only according to our instructions.

6. Data Security Measures

We implement comprehensive technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data in accordance with GDPR Article 32:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data at rest
  • AWS Cognito for secure authentication and user management
  • Role-based access control limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure backup procedures with encryption
  • Employee training on data protection and confidentiality

7. Data Retention Periods

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Check-In Data (Name, Email, Phone, ID Document): Retained as required by German law for guest registration and tax purposes (typically 6-12 months after check-out)
  • Booking and Payment Records: 10 years (as required by German commercial and tax law - HGB, AO)
  • Email Verification Codes: Deleted immediately after successful verification or after 24 hours if unused
  • Account Data: Until account deletion is requested (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Technical Logs: 90 days for security purposes

After the retention period expires, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized unless longer retention is required by law or for legal defense purposes.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR): Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR): Limit how we use your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR): Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR): Withdraw consent for processing at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@harmonest.de. We will respond to your request within one month as required by GDPR.

9.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our services. For analytics cookies, we obtain your explicit consent before use.
