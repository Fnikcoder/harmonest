Calendar Management Guide

Understanding Calendar Views:

  • Green: Available dates for booking
  • Blue: Confirmed bookings
  • Yellow: Pending bookings
  • Red: Blocked/maintenance dates
  • Gray: Past dates

Best Practices:

  • Block dates in advance for maintenance or personal use
  • Keep availability updated to maximize bookings
  • Use different views (month/week/day) for different tasks
  • Check for conflicts before confirming bookings
